About Dexignare

Dexignare is an educational platform that helps designers and now developers find their niche and improvise on skills that they possess. Dexignare's focus is to:

Organize Events For YOU

We focus on bringing out speakers who are eloquent in the designing field and can provide their exquisite knowledge to those who participate. A few of our notable past events are - The Fundamentals of Design and the Various Career Opportunites, Graphic Design and Insights of Illustrator, and Deep Dive into the UX World.

Upskill Designers

We organise sessions that you can benefit from. What are the benefits, you ask? These events are focused to help in revamping, brushing up, learning design. We have an Instagram page and Telegram group that you can be a part of for further details on upcoming events.

Create a space to express creativity

We believe that artists should have a platform where they are respected for their art. Our blog and Instagram feed are to feature designers who are willing to participate in our little contests, resulting in getting their work featured and even an opportunity to become a part of the Dexignare team!
